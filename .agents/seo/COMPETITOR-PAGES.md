## 5. Internal Linking Strategy for Comparison Pages

- Every comparison page links to → relevant service page + relevant case study
- Service pages link to → "See how we compare" (comparison hub)
- Blog posts mention competitors naturally → link to comparison pages
- FAQ page links to → comparison pages for "how are you different?" type questions
- Homepage "How it works" → links to service pages → links to industry pages
